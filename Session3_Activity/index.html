Sailor Moon, or more commonly known as Pretty Soldier Sailor Moon in Japan is an anime series that ran on Japanese television from March 7, 1992, to February 8, 1997, on Asahi Television. The series spanned 200 episodes, three feature films, five specials, and five memorials. Following this series were two reboots: one live-action tv show and later Sailor Moon Crystal.

The first season of the 90s anime features the first five sailor guardians, also known as The Inner Sailor Guardians

Synopsis

Usagi Tsukino is an average student and crybaby klutz who constantly scores low on her tests. Unexpectedly, her humdrum life is turned upside down when she saves a cat with a crescent moon on its head from danger. The cat, named Luna, later reveals that their meeting was not an accident: Usagi is destined to become Sailor Moon, a planetary guardian with the power to protect the Earth. Given a special brooch that allows her to transform, she must use her new powers to save the city from evil energy-stealing monsters sent by the malevolent Queen Beryl of the Dark Kingdom. But getting accustomed to her powers and fighting villains are not the only things she has to worry about. She must find the lost princess of the Moon Kingdom, the other Sailor Guardians, and the Legendary Silver Crystal in order to save the planet from destruction.

Sailor Moon

Also known as Usagi Tsukino, is the titular protagonist of the 90s anime, Sailor Moon. She was originally from a prosperous civilization on the Moon also known as the Moon Kingdom and was reborn on Earth and re-awoke as the leader of the Sailor Senshi

Sailor Mercury

Also known as Ami Mizuno, is one of the five original Inner Sailor Guardians of the Solar System, and was the 2nd Sailor Guardian introduced. Her powers are based around water and ice, as well as intelligence and computers.

Sailor Venus

Also known as Minako Aino, and Sailor V, is the present-day incarnation of Sailor Venus. She is the 5th Sailor Senshi to be introduced and join the Sailor team. She is also the real leader of the Inner Sailor Senshi. Minako became a Sailor Senshi by Artemis before Usagi.

Sailor Mars

Also known as Rei Hino, is the present-day incarnation of Sailor Mars. Rei lives at Hikawa Shrine with her grandfather and works as a Miko, befriending two crows named Phobos and Deimos. She would develop psychic powers that she would cultivate by diving through the Sacred Fire at the shrine.

Sailor Jupiter

Also known as Makoto Kino, the present-day incarnation of Sailor Jupiter. She is the fourth Sailor Senshi to be introduced in the anime. She also practices karate since her specialty is her strength. If she feels weak, she puts in the effort to get stronger.
